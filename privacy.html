1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TT-Poster (“we”, “us”) processes personal data when you use our web-based tool to connect a TikTok account and upload/publish videos using TikTok’s official APIs (where available).

TT-Poster is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance. “TikTok” is a trademark of its respective owner.

2. Controller

Controller (Data Controller): TT-Poster

Contact: ttposter@gmx.de

If you are located in the EU/EEA, the GDPR applies to many processing activities described here.

3. Data We Process

3.1 Data you provide

  • Account/contact data you provide to us (e.g., your email address if you contact support).
  • Content & metadata you submit for posting (e.g., video file, caption text, hashtags, selected settings).

3.2 Data received from TikTok (after you connect your account)

When you connect via TikTok OAuth, TikTok may provide data depending on the scopes enabled for the app. Typical examples (depending on configuration) include:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., open_id / user identifier, display name, avatar).
  • Authorization tokens (access token, refresh token) required to perform actions you request.

3.3 Technical and log data

  • IP address, user agent, timestamps, approximate request metadata (e.g., pages/actions used).
  • Operational logs needed for security and debugging (e.g., error messages, upload status).

3.4 We do not intentionally collect sensitive data

Please do not submit sensitive personal data (e.g., health data) through TT-Poster.

5. TikTok API & OAuth Connection

5.1 How the connection works

TT-Poster uses TikTok’s authorization flow (OAuth) so you can grant permissions to TT-Poster. After successful authorization, TikTok provides tokens that allow TT-Poster to perform actions you explicitly request (such as uploading a video as a draft or posting, depending on your enabled permissions and TikTok’s product capabilities).

5.2 Tokens and scope limitation

  • We request only the scopes needed to provide the selected functionality.
  • Access tokens are used to call TikTok APIs on your behalf. Refresh tokens may be used to obtain new access tokens.

5.3 Revoking access

You can revoke TT-Poster’s access at any time in TikTok’s account settings (or wherever TikTok provides revocation controls). After revocation, TT-Poster can no longer act on your behalf via TikTok APIs.

5.4 TikTok as a separate controller

TikTok processes your data under its own privacy policy and terms. TT-Poster is not responsible for TikTok’s processing.

6. Cookies & Analytics

6.1 Essential cookies

We may use essential cookies or similar technologies to keep you logged in, protect against abuse, and maintain basic functionality.

6.2 Analytics (optional)

If we use analytics in the future, we will (where required) ask for consent before storing non-essential cookies and describe the provider and purpose here.

6.3 How to control cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

7. Sharing & Recipients

We share personal data only when necessary to operate the service:

  • TikTok: to perform the actions you request (upload/publish) and to use TikTok APIs.
  • Hosting/Infrastructure providers: to run the web app and store required data (e.g., logs, configuration).
  • Service providers (if used): e.g., email support provider, monitoring, or security tooling.
  • Authorities: if required by law or to protect rights and security.

We do not sell personal data.

8. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described above:

  • OAuth tokens: stored only as long as needed to provide the service or until access is revoked; deleted/invalidated when no longer required.
  • Videos: stored only for the time needed to transfer/upload to TikTok (and troubleshooting). After completion, temporary files are removed where possible.
  • Logs: retained for a limited period for security and debugging, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Support emails: retained as long as needed to handle your request and for reasonable follow-up.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including access controls, encryption in transit (HTTPS), and least-privilege access where feasible.

No system is 100% secure. Please protect your devices and accounts.
